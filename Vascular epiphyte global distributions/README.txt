This dataset was used in the analyses for the accompanying manuscript "Vascular epiphytes contribute disproportionately to global centres of plant diversity" in Global Biogeography and Ecology GEB-2021-0338. 

It includes all epiphyte species distributions, environnmental data, and the following meta information:

Header	Description
link_ID	botanical country ID
kingdom_WWF	biogeographic realm
family	plant family
species_full	plant species name
geo_entity	name of botanical country
confidence_GIFT/WCSP	confidence of checklist; 1 = complete or near complete checklist, 0 = incomplete checklist
confidence_Hassler	confidence of checklist; 1 = complete or near complete checklist, 0 = incomplete checklist
biome	dominant biome of botanical country
longitude	longitude
latitude	latitude
lat_absolute	absolute latitude (°)
hemisphere	hemisphere
area	area (km2)
TF_Miocene	extent of tropical forest biomes during the Miocene climate optimum  (km2)
TF_current	extent of tropical forest biomes (km2)
TF_LGM	extent of tropical forest biomes during the Last Glacial Maximum (km2)
prec	mean annual precipitation (mm)
PrecSeas	precipitation seasonality (coefficient of variation in precipitation)
temp_mean	mean daily minimum temperature (°C)
ElevRange	elevational range (m)
LGM_ice	Last Glacial Maximum ice cover (km2)
vascular_total	total number of vascular plants
ferns_total	total number of pteridophytes
seeds_total	total number of seed plants
